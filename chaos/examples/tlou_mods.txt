129. Reduced Enemy Perception: Finally, clickers are truly blind.
130. Restart Checkpoint: Best served prior to the end of a long encounter.

132. Select Delay: Introduces a 5 second delay to selecting weapons or consumables
133. Sepia: Render Mode
134. Shape Shuffle: Randomly remaps the shape buttons (square, circle, triangle, X)
135. Shooting Delay: Introduces a 0.5 second delay to shooting and throwing
136. Show Pickups: Enables the HUD that shows notifications on ammo, crafting, and other pickups
137. Sideways Moonwalk: Go left to go right and go right to go left
138. Slow Motion: The speed-run killer
139. Snapshot: You can only aim for 1 second at a time, so take that shot quickly.
140. Swap D-Pad/Left Joystick: Retro-style motion and an odd analog inventory selector
141. Swap Joysticks: You may want to cross your thumbs to work with your muscle memory
142. Swap Shapes/Right Joystick: Analog actions and digital camera movement
143. TLOU1 Run Glitch: Is this an Any% speedrun of TLOU1? Rapid D-pad circular presses. Try moving while prone.
144. Terminated: Render Mode
145. Toss A Molly/Bomb: Time to riot. Chucks a molly (for Ellie) or bomb (for Abby).
146. Touch Of Death: Golden fists activated: one-hit enemy deaths
147. Touchpad Aiming: No more aiming with the joystick. Finally making use of the touchpad!
148. Traversal Assistance: Climb when you really do not want to
149. Trigger Remap: Randomly remaps trigger/bumper buttons (L1, R1, L2, R2)
150. Trinity: Render Mode
151. Use Items: Shoots or throws 6 of whatever item is currently equipped. No effect for medkits.
