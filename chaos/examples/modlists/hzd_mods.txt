The following modifiers are available for Horizon Zero Dawn (PS4):

1. Aim Delay: Introduces a 1 second delay to aiming
2. Aim In Place: Movement is disabled if aiming
3. Bad Stamina: Running is disabled after 2 seconds and takes 4 seconds to recharge.
4. Chaos: Applies 5 random modifiers
5. Controller Flip: Up is down. Joysticks and D-pad inverted vertially, L1 swapped with L2, Triangle swapped with X, etc.
6. Controller Mirror: Left and right joysticks are swapped. R2 is now L2. Circle is D-pad left, etc.
7. Criss-Cross Joysticks: Swap vertical and horizontal joystick axes. Up and down are now left and right.
8. D-pad Rotate: Rotates the D-pad inputs clockwise
9. Desk Pop: We honor the flag, and you crap on it when you don't shoot your gun https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wWZTTtE5_zQ
10. Disable Camera: Good luck with aiming and looking around!
11. Disable D-pad: Inventory selection is disabled
12. Dodge Delay: Introduces a 0.5 second delay to dodging
13. Double Tap: It's Rule #2. Everytime a shot is fired, another occurs in quick succession. For Rule #1, see Leeroy Jenkins.
14. Drunk: Random joystick motion. Also don't push things too far, or you may stumble (go prone)
15. Factions Pro: Keeping things toxic with teabagging
16. Force Aiming: The aiming button is held
17. Force Running: To move is to run (mostly)
18. Hoarder: Cannot use bombs or traps
19. Input Delay: Introduces a 0.2 second delay to the controller
20. Inverted: Inverts the vertical axis for aiming
21. Joystick Delay: Introduces a 0.2 second delay to movement and aiming.
22. Jump Delay: Introduces a 2.0 second delay to jump presses
23. Keep Dodging: Repeaated dodges. Don't stand too near a ledge
24. Keep Jumping: Repeated presses of jump
25. Leeroy Jenkins: Alright let's do this! LEEEEROOOOOY NNNJEEEENNKIINNS!!! (Holds forward and sprint)
26. Literally Melee Only: Nothing but melee. Literally
27. Max Sensitivity: Goodbye precision aiming. Joystick postions multiplied by 5.
28. Mega Scope Sway: Good luck landing shots
29. Melee Delay: Introduces a 0.5 second delay to melee presses
30. Melee Only: No projectile weapons, and no inventory items
31. Min Sensitivity: It's like slomo, but not for the enemies. Joystick positions divided by 2.5.
32. Moonwalk: Be like Michael Jackson! Trying to walk forward will actually make you go backward.
33. Moose: The moose is dead. The moose does not move. (Disables movement)
34. Motion Control Aiming: No more aiming with the joystick. Tilt the controller to aim/look instead.
35. Motion Control Movement: No more movement with the joystick. Tilt the controller to move instead.
36. Mystery: Applies a random modifier
37. Nascar: There is no going right in Nascar. Right movement/camera disabled
38. No Aiming Camera: Camera is disabled if aiming
39. No Backward Movement: Moving backwards is not allowed
40. No Crouching: And you thought you were going to sneak through this area
41. No Dodge Roll: Stand and fight!
42. No Forward Movement: Moving forward is not allowed
43. No Grab: Swiper no swiping! No looting or interacting with anything. Say goodbye to the everything button.
44. No Horizontal Aim: The horizontal camera is disabled
45. No Jumping: No climbing or jumping
46. No Multi-Shot: Double and triple shots are disabled
47. No Reload/Shoot: Can't reload or draw the bow while aiming.
48. No Slow-Mo: No slow motion. Aiming is still allowed
49. No Strafing: Forwards/back movement is disabled. Only side-to-side motion is allowed.
50. No Weapon Wheel: You can't open the weapon wheel
51. Only Aim Movement: Movement is disabled if not aiming
52. Only Strafe Movement: Side-to-side movement is disabled. Only forward/back motion is allowed.
53. Pacifist%: No shooting or melee.
54. Periodic Focus Mode: Stop, look, listen to your heart, hear what it's saying.
55. Rachy Flail: The only way to melee is to follow the master techniques of RachyMonster
56. Random Movement: Random joystick motion
57. Random Remap: Randomly remaps buttons (square, circle, triangle, X, L1, R1, L2, R2, L3, R3)
58. Rubbernecking: Woah! What's behind you? Invokes a periodic, 180-degree quick turn.
59. Select Delay: Introduces a 5 second delay to selecting weapons or consumables
60. Shape Shuffle: Randomly remaps the shape buttons (square, circle, triangle, X)
61. Shooting Delay: Introduces a 0.5 second delay to shooting
62. Sideways Moonwalk: Go left to go right and go right to go left
63. Snapshot: You can only aim for 1 second at a time, so take that shot quickly.
64. Stumblebum: Fast movement will make you go prone
65. Swap D-Pad/Left Joystick: Retro-style motion and an odd analog inventory selector
66. Swap Joysticks: You may want to cross your thumbs to work with your muscle memory
67. Swap Shapes/Right Joystick: Analog actions and digital camera movement
68. Touchpad Aiming: No more aiming with the joystick. Finally making use of the touchpad!
69. Trigger Remap: Randomly remaps trigger/bumper buttons (L1, R1, L2, R2)
70. Use Items: Shoots or throws 6 of whatever item is currently equipped. No effect for medkits.
71. Zoolander: We are not an ambiturner, but at least we can look right. Left movement/camera disabled.
